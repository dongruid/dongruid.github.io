Research Interests

Representational Properties of Neural Codes: Investigating geometric properties of neural representations that facilitate generalization in natural and artificial intelligence. Focus on how intelligent agents create internal representations of the world to predict the future and learn in novel situations.

Data Science Applied to Real-world Challenges: Applying statistical modeling and machine learning to extract insights from complex, high-dimensional data. Specializing in analyzing noisy but structured datasets, with interest in user analytics, marketing, and related domains.

Applications in Vision and Language: Exploring how principles from neuroscience and machine learning can provide insights to better understand, enhance, and ensure the safety of state-of-the-art vision and language models.
